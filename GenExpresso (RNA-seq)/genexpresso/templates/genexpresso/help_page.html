Getting Started

Hi! Here are some guides to help you navigate through GenExpresso.

Step 1: Create a New Project

To start analyzing data, create a new project. Go to the Projects page and click "Add New Project".

Step 2: Add a Dataset

Inside your project, you can fetch data from GEO. Click "Add New Dataset" and fill in the necessary information (GEO ID).

Step 3: Analyze Your Data

Once you have datasets added, you can analyze them by clicking "Analyze Dataset". We will process the data and visualize the results. You will find three plots and able to interact with it.

Step 4: Manage Your Projects

You can view, edit, or delete datasets and projects at any time. Use the "Edit" and "Delete" buttons in your project view.

Need More Help?

If you have questions or issues, feel free to reach out via support@genexpresso.com.
